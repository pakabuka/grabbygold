The core of Grabby Gold is its simple, addictive gameplay loop:

  • 1.
    TIME YOUR SWING: The claw swings back and forth. Tap at the perfect moment to lower it.
  • 2.
    GRAB THE TREASURE: Aim for gold, diamonds, and mysterious goodie bags. Bigger items are heavier!
  • 3.
    BEAT THE CLOCK: Reach the level goal before time runs out to advance and earn your reward!
